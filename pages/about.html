About Shree Namrata Dress and Tailor

Founded in 2000, Shree Namrata Dress and Tailor has established itself as a cornerstone of quality and craftsmanship in Shyam Nagar, Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. With over two decades of experience, we have become synonymous with excellence in tailoring, specializing in school uniforms and a variety of civil garments. AA Legacy of Quality

Under the leadership of Mr. Kunwar Pal Singh, Shree Namrata Dress and Tailor has grown from a humble beginning into a well-respected name in the tailoring industry. Mr. Singh, with his vast experience and unwavering commitment to quality, has been the driving force behind our success. His vision has always been clear: to offer garments that are not only comfortable and durable but also reflect the personality and style of the wearer. Our shop is renowned for its attention to detail, ensuring that every stitch is perfect, and every garment fits flawlessly. This commitment to quality has earned us a loyal customer base in Shyam Nagar and beyond, making us a preferred destination for those seeking well-made school uniforms and other custom-tailored garments.

Specialization in School Uniforms

One of our core specialties at Shree Namrata Dress and Tailor is the creation of school uniforms. We understand the importance of uniforms in a student’s life; they are not just clothing but a representation of the school’s identity and values. Our uniforms are designed to be both practical and comfortable, allowing students to focus on their studies without distraction. We work closely with schools to ensure that our uniforms meet the specific requirements of each institution. From fabric selection to the final fitting, we take pride in producing uniforms that are durable, easy to maintain, and tailored to perfection. Our uniforms are not only a testament to our craftsmanship but also to our understanding of the needs of students and schools alike.

Expertise in Civil Garments

In addition to school uniforms, Shree Namrata Dress and Tailor also excels in the creation of civil garments. Whether you need a custom-tailored suit, a traditional outfit for a special occasion, or everyday wear that fits just right, we have the expertise to deliver. Our range of civil garments is as diverse as our clientele, and we cater to all tastes and preferences. Each garment is crafted with the same level of care and precision that has become our hallmark. We use only the highest quality fabrics, ensuring that our clothing is not only stylish but also long-lasting. Our customers know that when they choose Shree Namrata Dress and Tailor, they are choosing garments that are made to measure, made to last, and made to impress.
